Background

Trippers Mission: Catch a Hope is a mobile side scroller puzzle game in space. As part of my final project at Universidad de Monterrey and in conjunction with the Playful Interactive art and development teams, I collaborated as a concept artist, UI designer, and Graphic Designer during the development of Trippers.
